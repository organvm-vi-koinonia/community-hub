The Eight-Organ System

ORGAN-VI sits at the center of a creative infrastructure spanning theory (I), art (II), engineering (III), governance (IV), methodology (V), community (VI), distribution (VII), and meta-coordination (VIII). This hub is where ideas from all organs converge through human dialogue.

  • I · Theoria — Recursive systems, ontology, epistemology
  • II · Poiesis — Generative arts, performance, AI collaboration
  • III · Ergon — Products, SaaS, data tools, gaming
  • IV · Taxis — Governance, orchestration, multi-agent systems
  • V · Logos — Essays, methodology, building in public
  • VI · Koinonia — Salons, reading groups, mentorship
  • VII · Kerygma — Distribution, social automation, outreach
  • VIII · Meta — Registry, constitution, cross-organ coordination
